How It Works

The process.

A clear, structured approach from first conversation to final delivery, so you always know where we are and what comes next.

Please note: the process may vary slightly depending on your chosen package.

01

Getting to know you and your brand

Questionnaire & Onboarding

The process begins with an online meet and greet where we'll discuss your goals, expectations, and overall vision for the brand. You'll complete a structured brand questionnaire that helps define your audience, positioning, and visual direction.

This step ensures clarity from the start and sets a strong foundation for a focused and efficient design process.

Work begins once the 50% deposit invoice has been settled.

02

Defining the visual direction

Moodboards

Using your responses and insights from our initial discussion and questionnaire, I'll develop curated moodboards that define the creative direction for your logo and brand.

03

From ideas to identity

Concepts & Final Logo

Once the direction is approved, I'll develop a range of custom logo concepts for you to review and ultimately select your brand's final logo.

04

Building your visual system

Brand Identity

Once your logo is finalised, I'll develop a complete brand identity system that ensures consistency across all platforms.

This includes logo usage rules, colour palette, typography system, layout guidance, and supporting visual direction to maintain a cohesive and professional brand presence.

05

Bringing your brand to life

Collateral

This stage focuses on real-world application of your identity. I'll design branded assets based on your needs, which may include business cards, letterheads, packaging, social media templates, email signatures, or other key touchpoints that help your brand show up consistently and professionally.

06

Your brand, ready for the world

Final Deliverables

All approved assets are exported in industry-standard formats including PDF, PNG, JPG, SVG and EPS, and delivered in organised folders for both print and digital use.

Everything is structured to ensure your brand is easy to apply, scalable, and ready for immediate implementation across any platform.

Final files are released once the remaining 50% balance has been settled.
